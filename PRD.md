# Product Requirements Document: Ladera Women's Golf & Social Platform

## 1. Project Overview
A marketing and appointment booking web application for the **Ladera Golf Course** in Albuquerque, New Mexico. The platform focuses on a premium, welcoming instructional golf program tailored specifically for women looking to improve their game, build community, and network.

* **Lead Instructor:** David Lovato
* **Location:** Ladera Golf Course, Albuquerque, NM (Featuring views of the Sandia Mountains)
* **Core Philosophy:** Combining high-quality athletic instruction with a relaxed, inclusive social environment.

## 2. Target Audience
* Women professionals, beginners, and intermediate golfers in the Albuquerque area.
* Users looking for low-pressure sports education combined with community building.

## 3. Core Features & Business Logic
1.  **Marketing & Landing Page:** High-converting landing page highlighting the program benefits, schedule, Albuquerque setting, and David Lovato’s coaching credentials.
2.  **Instructional Booking System (Strict Constraints):**
    * **Private Lessons:** Limited strictly to **one (1) private 1-on-1 lesson per user per calendar month** to ensure fair availability.
    * **Group Sessions:** Regularly scheduled recurring clinics (e.g., bi-weekly Saturdays) with open capacity limits up to 12 participants.
3.  **Weekly Social Gatherings Manager:**
    * An RSVP system for weekly post-lesson or standalone social hours at the Ladera Clubhouse/Patio.
4.  **User Authentication:** Simple signup/login for managing bookings, RSVPs, and profile details.

## 4. Media & Asset Strategy
* **Imagery:** Use premium golf imagery with high-contrast NM desert sunset lighting.
* **Instructor Assets:** Placeholder assets for David Lovato (`/assets/david-lovato-bio.jpg`, `/assets/david-instructional.jpg`). *Note: Content and explicit consent pending from David Lovato.*
